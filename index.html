What is Re-Dream?

I'm sure you have something that you've been wanting to make for a while, I know I do! This is your chance to finally work on that project that's been on the backburner!! 🔥

The purpose of Re-Dream is to work on something you're truly passionate about and we will ship you a way to make it reality and publish your work!

This is also presented as an opportunity to remake old projects! Not only are you encouraged to make a new project you're passionate about but you're also encouraged to breathe new life into old projects! 🤡

What Projects Can You Ship?

🎉 ANY SOFTWARE YOU'RE PASSIONATE ABOUT! 🎉

  • A game you've always wanted to make
  • A useful script or tool
  • A mobile or web app
  • An old project you're proud of
  • Almost anything you can dream up!

What Will You Get in Return?

Publishing Licenses

Get licenses to publish your game to Steam, Android, iOS, Microsoft, and more (where applicable)!

Custom Domains

Domains to host and showcase your projects to the world

And More!

If you don't want to publish your project, you can simply ask for a grant toward something you want, or have always wanted!
